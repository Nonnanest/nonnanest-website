About Nonnanest

Nonnanest, Inc. is a baby wellness technology company based in Charleston, SC, founded in 2024 by Nathan Overbey. The company's flagship product, SightAware™ by Nonnanest, is a contactless infrared baby wellness monitor that reads skin temperature from up to four feet away without requiring a wearable, WiFi, or a smartphone app. SightAware communicates via FHSS (Frequency Hopping Spread Spectrum), a secure wireless protocol that keeps all data local between the nursery camera and a dedicated 5-inch HD parent display. Patent pending. Priced at $329 with no subscription fees.

Founder

Nathan Overbey, Founder & CEO

Nathan designed SightAware after realizing no baby monitor on the market could track skin temperature contactlessly. He brings over 15 years of enterprise technology experience from Salesforce, ServiceNow, and Blackbaud, and holds an MBA from the University of South Carolina. Nathan is a father of two and the product's first real-world user.

Founder quotes

Journalists are welcome to use these quotes attributed to Nathan Overbey, Founder & CEO, Nonnanest.

On the origin

When our son was six months old, he woke up in the middle of the night warm, and all the monitor could tell me was the room temperature. I thought, how long had his skin temperature been rising? That was the moment I realized the market needed more awareness, not another camera.

Nathan Overbey, Founder & CEO, Nonnanest
On privacy

We made a deliberate decision to keep SightAware offline. Parents are concerned about connected devices and security risks. We built it on FHSS, a fully encrypted signal that never touches the internet. Private by design, not by promise.

Nathan Overbey, Founder & CEO, Nonnanest
On the approach

We're not trying to replace a parent's instinct. We're giving that instinct better information. SightAware is a gentle layer of awareness that works quietly in the background while your baby sleeps.

Nathan Overbey, Founder & CEO, Nonnanest
